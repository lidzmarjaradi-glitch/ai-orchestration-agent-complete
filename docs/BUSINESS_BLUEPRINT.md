# Business Blueprint

## Positioning
AI workflow agents that coordinate multiple models to complete real work iteratively until the user is satisfied.

## First Market
Non-technical founders who want to build software MVPs.

## Problem
AI tools give incomplete or inconsistent outputs. Users do not know how to drive the work to completion.

## Solution
A controlled loop: Planner → Executor → Evaluator → Next Prompt.

## Differentiation
Structured iteration, acceptance criteria, persistent task history, strict evaluation, and human approval checkpoints.
